How do I order?

To order your food:

  1. We require you to login for security purposes.
  2. Click on the restaurant you would like to order from.
  3. Select a dish or food from the restaurant.
  4. Finally check out~ And wait for a few minutes for your food to arrive!
Can I cancel my order if I have made one?

Yes, it is possible! You may only cancel if your dish is in preparation and not during delivery.

How long does my membership last?

A membership payment lasts an entire full month(30 days) from your last payment.
